Cheeseburger

Savor the classic American flavor with our delicious cheeseburger recipe. It's a timeless favorite for any burger enthusiast.

Ingredients:

  • Ground beef
  • Hamburger buns
  • Cheese slices (e.g., cheddar, American)
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Sliced tomatoes
  • Sliced onions
  • Pickles
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Preheat your grill or stovetop skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Divide the ground beef into patties, season with salt and pepper, and cook on the grill or skillet until desired doneness is reached.
  3. Toast the hamburger buns on the grill or in a toaster until lightly browned.
  4. Assemble the burgers by placing a patty on the bottom half of each bun.
  5. Add a slice of cheese on top of each patty, allowing it to melt from the heat.
  6. Top with lettuce, tomatoes, onions, and pickles.
  7. Spread ketchup and mustard on the top half of the buns and place them on top of the burger assembly.
  8. Serve your cheeseburgers hot with your favorite sides. Enjoy!
